Hinweise zur Anwendung von Anzeigeröhren - Sonderausgabe 3/1968

Date [ 20 Nov 2024 ] Categories [ documents ]

English title: Notes on the use of indicator tubes

This document contains an excerpt from the magazine “radio und fernsehen” (“radio and television”) published in the GDR relating to the use of various East German display tubes but mainly focusing on different use cases for the Z570M. It was issued by RFT in March 1968.
